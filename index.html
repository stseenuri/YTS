About Thanu

Village heart, city studio

From courtyards and charpais to Zoom screens, Thanu bridges the warmth of village life with the clarity of modern teaching.

Thanu grew up around open skies, verandahs, and slow mornings where simple stretches, chai, and conversations blended into one quiet ritual. Yoga by Thanu brings that feeling into city lives.

Expect soft cues, a mix of Indian and global music, and plenty of options if you’re stiff, recovering, or starting for the very first time.

  • Beginner friendly with variations for knees, hips, and back.
  • Small, cozy batches so you’re seen, not lost in a crowd.
  • Seasonal practices that follow Indian rhythms — summer cooling, monsoon grounding, festive unwinds.
Classes from a calm, courtyard-inspired space in Hyderabad with plants, mitti tones, and space for your mat and mind.

Perfect! 60% of our students are complete beginners. We design every session around YOUR current fitness level—not some impossible standard you need to reach. All ages and fitness levels welcome.

How is this different from YouTube videos?

YouTube gives you the same routine as everyone else. We give you poses specifically chosen for YOUR condition. Plus live feedback so you don't injure yourself with incorrect form.

I have PCOD/back pain. Will this actually help?

Our therapeutic protocols are built from 5+ years of practice helping people with specific conditions. Check Chandana's and Vaishnavi's testimonials above—real PCOD results in 30-60 days. Same for back pain and sleep issues.

Every session is recorded. Watch anytime that fits your schedule. But we strongly recommend live classes for real-time corrections and personalized guidance.

Not at all! While many of our students are women dealing with PCOD, we have plenty of male students working on back pain, flexibility, stress management, and overall wellness. Everyone is welcome.

Absolutely! Understanding instructions in your mother tongue helps you focus on the practice—not translation. Choose your comfortable language during signup.

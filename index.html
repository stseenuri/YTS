About Thanu

Village heart, city studio

Mixing the warmth of a village verandah with the clarity of modern teaching, Thanu keeps yoga honest, gentle, and deeply human.

Thanu grew up around courtyards, open skies, and slow mornings where stretching, chai, and chatter blended into one quiet ritual. Yoga by Thanu brings that feeling into everyday city life.

Expect soft cues, desi playlists, and plenty of options if you’re stiff, recovering, or just starting. This is not a performative studio; this is practice that can stay with you like home.

  • Beginner friendly, with adaptations for knees, hips, and back.
  • Small, cozy batches for individual attention and safer postures.
  • Seasonal practices inspired by Indian rhythms — from monsoon grounding to festive unwinds.
Classes from a calm, courtyard-inspired space in Hyderabad, with plants, mitti tones, and space to breathe.

About Thanu

Village heart,
city studio

Thanu grew up around courtyards, open skies, and slow mornings where simple stretches, chai, and quiet conversations blended into one ritual. Yoga by Thanu brings that feeling into modern city lives.

Expect soft cues, a mix of Indian and global music, and plenty of options if you're stiff, recovering, or starting for the first time. This isn't a performance studio — it's practice that can stay with you like home.

  • Beginner friendly with variations for knees, hips, and back.
  • Small, cozy batches for individual attention and safer postures.
  • Seasonal practices inspired by Indian rhythms — summer cooling, monsoon grounding, festive unwinds.

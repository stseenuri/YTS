About

Meet Thanuja

I am Thanuja, a certified yoga practitioner with a 200-hour Yoga Teacher Training certification, Therapeutic Yoga training, and a Diploma in Yoga.

I have been practicing yoga for over 5 years and teaching for nearly a year, guiding individuals through mindful, condition-based yoga practices. Over this period, I have taught 100+ students through both online and offline sessions from my home studio, offering personalized attention and consistent support.

My expertise lies in therapeutic yoga, with a strong focus on back pain management, sleep-related issues, women's hormonal health like PCOD and PCOS, and menstrual wellness. I specialize in creating customized yoga protocols based on individual needs, combining asana, pranayama, and relaxation techniques for real, lasting results.

I strongly believe in yoga as a holistic healing system, not just a physical practice. My approach emphasizes breath awareness, mindful movement, and deep relaxation, helping students restore balance, improve body awareness, and build long-term well-being.

Through my teachings, I help individuals move toward pain-free movement, better sleep, hormonal balance, and inner harmony, supporting the body, mind, and soul.
